    Family of equivalences

    We have a family of maps $f : \Pi_{(x:A)} B(x) \rightarrow C(x)$,

    if for each $x : A$, $f(x)$ is an equivalence, we say that $f$ is a family of equivalences.

    This happens iff $tot(f)$ is an equivalence.
